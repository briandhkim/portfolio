The CubeTube

Designed and developed to
provide the best viewing experience.

The CubeTube is a content management application aimed to provide an optimal viewing experience for YouTube users.

About the app

The CubeTube provides a way for the viewer to keep track of his or her channel subscription anonymously. Using a uniquely generated link, the viewer can keep track of channel content without using an account requring an ID and a password.

The channel subscription can also be organized using custom categories of the viewer's own creation.

Why?

YouTube provides a wonderful platform where people can show and voice themselves to the world. However, even YouTube has some features that are lacking. We wanted to contribute in addressing some of those issues to make the users' viewing experience even better.

Features

anonymous subscription tracking, channel subscription categorization, theater mode, easy video queue, and more...
